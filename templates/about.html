Conference Bingo!

Rules

  • When a speaker at the conference says or shows a phrase on a slide, click on the square to mark the term (click again to unmark).
  • The bingo term must be said by the speaker (not by questioners; speaker's response to questions is OK).
  • Speaker does not get credit for his or her own buzzwords.
  • Card is saved automatically at every click (stored under the email address used).
  • Get 5 in a row (vertical, horizontal, diagonal) and you win! Announce your winning a bingo card on Twitter using the conference hashtag and/or #bingo.
  • Bonus points for weaving the word "bingo" into a question to the speaker.

Privacy policy

Your email address will not be used for any purposes other than storing your bingo card, and aggregated statistics on bingo players at each conference.
